Xtrackers Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Xtrackers is a local-first Chrome extension that analyzes X/Twitter follower changes entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Xtrackers does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data (follower lists, scan history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

Permissions

storage: To save follower data and preferences locally on your device.

host permissions (x.com, twitter.com): To read follower and following lists in read-only mode. The extension does not write, click, or perform any action on your behalf.
